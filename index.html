About Me

What am I about

My name is Nicholas Geary and I am a problem solver. Being able to adapt, is how I've found what I consider success.

I started my career as a CAD designer in manufacturing after completing an associate degree in Computer Aided Design and Drafting from Erie Community College. This then led me to further my education and earn a bachelor's degree in Industrial and Systems Engineering from the University at Buffalo. After working in manufacturing for several years; holding a variety of roles including CNC Operator, CAD Designer, and Industrial Engineer; I have found myself in a new role as a Data Analysis on the Operational Analytics team at a health insurance company.

Over the past couple of years, I've found myself interested in the world of advanced data analytics and data science. With a focus on operational optimization.
